ARTWORK

车窗下的她

She sits beneath the car window, draped in a vibrant crimson scarf. The young woman appears contemplative, with a hint of perplexity, as she finds herself in a foreign city. Her gaze is fixed ahead, reflecting a mix of inner solitude and anticipation, along with contemplation and longing for the future.




EXPERIENCE

When creating an oil painting, I express my profound experiences of natural beauty or human subjects through colors and lines. Alternatively, when depicting urban scenes, I infuse my observations and reflections on city life, enhancing the artwork's expressive power and emotional resonance.
