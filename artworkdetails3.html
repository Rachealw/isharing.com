IMPRINT OF THE SEA


This is a serene and mysterious ocean. The surface of the sea is calm, like a mirror reflecting the tired countenance of the sky. The beach is covered with fine grains of sand, gently brushed by the waves, leaving behind a trail of textures, marking the merging of the ocean and the land. In the sky, dark clouds gather, burying the sunlight, creating an elusive atmosphere of mystery.
